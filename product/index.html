Platform Trust

Built with Verification and Trust in Mind.

Every submission goes through a verification layer designed to protect brands and reward quality participation.

AI-Assisted Verification

Every screenshot submission is analyzed automatically before brand review — checking for authenticity signals and quality indicators.

Handle Matching

Connected social accounts are matched against submitted screenshots to verify the action came from a real, linked profile.

Submission Quality Checks

Submissions are reviewed for completeness, relevance, and quality before credits are released to the Swrmer's wallet.

Comment Liveness Verification

Comments are checked to confirm they remain live on the platform at review time — not deleted after submission.

Account Protection Systems

Bot detection runs on every submission. Accounts that consistently fail quality checks are flagged and removed from the platform — protecting brands and rewarding Swrmers who participate honestly.
