FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this just bots? ＋

No. Every submission requires a screenshot from a real account. Brands review work before credits release. Bot detection runs on every action.

How do payouts work? ＋

Verified submissions release credits to your wallet. Cash out to PayPal once you hit the $20 minimum - no waiting weeks, no gift cards.

What does it cost brands? ＋

Brands fund campaigns with credits and set a per-task rate. You only pay for completed, verified work.

Which platforms are supported? ＋

Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X / Twitter, and Threads - with more on the way. Anything that takes a screenshot, we can run a campaign for.

Is Swrm new? ＋

New build, battle-tested playbook. Modern stack, fast payouts, real screenshot proof.
