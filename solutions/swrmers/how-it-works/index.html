How it works

From Signup
to Cashout.

Here's exactly how earning on SWRM works — no surprises, no hidden steps.

Start earning → See how it works
1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email in under a minute. It's completely free — no subscription, no credit card, no catch. Just an account.

Takes about 30 seconds
2

Browse Open Campaigns

See all currently live campaigns from real brands. Filter by platform (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, Threads), task type (follow, like, share, comment), or payout rate. Pick what works for you.

By platform By task type By payout
3

Pick a Campaign

Read what the campaign asks. The task, the platform, the rate per completion — it's all visible before you commit. If it works for you, take it. If not, skip it. No obligation.

4

Complete the Task

Head to the platform and do the action — follow the account, like the post, share to your story, or leave a comment. You stay on the platform the whole time. SWRM never accesses your accounts directly.

Follow ＋ Like ♡ Share ↗ Comment ❝
5

Submit Your Proof

Take a screenshot showing the completed action and upload it through SWRM. Submissions are reviewed quickly — most are approved within 24 hours.

Most approvals within 24 hours
6

Earn Credits

Once your submission is approved, the task's credit value lands in your SWRM wallet. Complete more tasks, stack more credits. You can see your balance in real time.

7

Cash Out to PayPal

Once your balance hits $20, you can request a cashout to PayPal. No waiting for monthly payment windows. No gift cards. No conversion games. Just real money in your PayPal account.

$20 minimum · PayPal only · anytime

Trust & safety

How We Keep It Real.

Brands Pay Upfront

Campaigns are funded before they go live. You'll never complete a task that isn't backed by real credits.

Screenshot Proof

Every submission is screenshot-verified. No black boxes. No unverifiable clicks.

Bot Detection

Every action is checked. Suspicious submissions are flagged. Real accounts only — always.

No Account Access

SWRM never connects to or accesses your social accounts. Only your screenshots are used.
