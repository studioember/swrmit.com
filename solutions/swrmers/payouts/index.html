Timing

When You Get Paid.

After Submission Approval

Credits appear in your wallet as soon as your submission is approved. Most approvals happen within 24 hours. You'll see your balance update in real time.

Once you hit the $20 minimum, you can request a PayPal transfer whenever you want. No monthly windows. No queues. Cash out on your schedule.

Trust & safety

How We Protect You.

Brands Pay Upfront

Campaigns are funded before they go live. You'll never complete work that isn't already paid for.

No Account Access

SWRM never connects to or logs into your social accounts. Only your screenshots are used for verification.

Bot Detection on Every Task

Every submission is reviewed. Suspicious activity gets flagged. Real accounts only. That's the only way this works.

You Skip Anything

No campaign is mandatory. If a task doesn't feel right, skip it. No penalty, no explanation needed.

Rate Shown Before You Commit

You see exactly what a task pays before you take it. No hidden rates, no bait-and-switch.

Free to Join, Always

No subscription. No sign-up fee. No pay-to-earn scheme. SWRM is free and earns you money, not the other way around.
